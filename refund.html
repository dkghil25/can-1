Refund & Return Policy

At Seravita Botanicals, our small-batch creations are crafted with care. As our products are made in limited runs and often sell out quickly, we currently do not accept returns or exchanges once an order has been confirmed.

Refunds

If your order was placed in error or you experience an issue with your purchase, please contact us within 7 days of your order. Refunds will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

Cancellations

If you wish to cancel your order before it ships, contact us as soon as possible. Once an item has been dispatched, we cannot guarantee cancellation.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If a product arrives damaged or incorrect, please email us a photo and order number within 5 days of delivery. We’ll work to make it right promptly.

Out-of-Stock Items

Due to our limited batches, some products may sell out. You can join the waitlist to be notified of restocks or new collection releases.
